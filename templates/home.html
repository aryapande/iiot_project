Lucknow Central

Experience the utmost craftsmanship from the folks of Central, where our chefs, bartenders and sommeliers bring you on a journey through Central’s kaleidoscope of flavours.


The impression is really like being at home, welcomed by old friends who tell you about the origin of each dish; you can watch the cooks work through a large window overlooking the hall or decide to personally visit the kitchen: “It is not forbidden to enter the kitchen” is what the chefs like to say. Simplicity and genuineness are skills not to be hidden.
